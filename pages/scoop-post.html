Basement Hi-Fi Night Recap

Basement Hi-Fi Night started as an excuse to drag the good speakers into the living room and ended up as a full-on weekly ritual. This week we spun Big Star, a deep-cut Tom Petty tune, and a brand-new Chicago band that sounds like they time-traveled from 1974.

The chat was packed with people sharing the records they grew up hearing around the house — everything from Steely Dan to The O’Jays to random soft-rock tapes from the glove compartment. Those stories are the best part of doing this: every record means something totally different to whoever’s listening.
